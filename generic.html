I’ve been chasing one move -- the Full Planche. Most people who are really serious about it take around 6 months to a year to get there. Me? I’ve been learning it for 9 years now. Follow Full Planche More > here.

There’s this high school kid I know -- he started training the Full Planche in grade 10. Now, just a year later, he’s already unlocked it. He’s still working on perfecting his form, but damn!! it’s impressive.

I want to nail this move too -- not just because it looks cool (which it totally does), but because I want to teach others how to get there. Maybe even master every move, every aspect -- training, nutrition, recovery -- and turn it all into a course someday.

So tell me... Would you be interested?

Get in touch

Ever since the Decathlon Free Gym shut down for renovation -- they say it’ll take about a year -- my crew and I have been training Calisthenics at the Phuket Police Gym instead. It’s not fancy, but it’s got the raw, old-school vibe that pushes you to grind harder every session. The bars are solid, the energy’s real, and the people here are all about progress. If you’re around Phuket and into Calisthenics (or just curious to try), come join us! We usually hit the gym around 6:30 PM and wrap up by 9:00 PM -- no fees, no egos, just pure training and good vibes..
